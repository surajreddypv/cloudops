*What is DevOps?
DevOps is a practice that is followed by the Development team and Operational team together from the beginning to the final stage of the project.
DevOps mainly focuses on automating and integrating all the stages to achieve faster and more reliable software delivery.

*What are the tools used in each stage of DevOps ?
1.Plan:-Jira,Confluence,Slack
2.Code:-GIT,Github,Gitlab,Bitbucket
3.Build:-Jenkins,Travis CI
4.Test:-Selenium,TestNG,JUnit
5.Deployment:-Docker,Kubernetes,Ansible,Puppet
6.Monitor:-Grafana,kibana
7.Feedback:-Slack,Email
8.Operate:-Kubernetes,Docker
